Welcome to Receipt Tracker, the ultimate app for managing your receipts!

Whether you're tracking expenses for travel, food, medical, or anything else, our app makes it simple to capture, organize, and claim your receipts.

Key Features:

  • Take Photos: Snap pictures of your receipts for easy storage.
  • Organize Receipts: Sort your receipts by category and date for quick access.
  • Categorize Expenses: Assign a category and label the amount.
  • Claim Receipts: Easily mark receipts as claimed to track your finances.
  • Simple Interface: Organize your receipts in a clear, user-friendly way.
  • Future JPA integration: API to communicate with JPA in future.
